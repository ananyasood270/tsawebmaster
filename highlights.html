Featured Community Resources

A closer look at organizations that support Baytown residents

Hearts and Hands of Baytown

Category: Food Support

Hearts and Hands of Baytown helps reduce hunger by providing food, nutritional education, and community support. They work with local partners to help families stretch their food budget.

Impact: Hosts food distributions and fresh market events for the community.

Phone: (832) 597-8908
Email: info@heartsandhandsofbaytown.com

Love Network of Baytown

Category: Basic Needs Support

The Love Network of Baytown provides food pantry services, help with utilities and rent, transportation assistance, and hygiene supplies for families in need.

Impact: Assists thousands of individuals and families each year with essential services.

Phone: (281) 422-5683
Email: admin@lovenetworkofbaytown.org

Houston's House of Hope

Category: Youth Support

Houston's House of Hope focuses on helping at-risk youth through mentoring, leadership activities, and enrichment programs that build confidence and life skills.

Impact: Provides positive opportunities and support for youth facing challenging situations.

Phone: (281) 837-7314
Email: info@houstonshoh.org
