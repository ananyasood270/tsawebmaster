About Baytown Community Hub

Helping connect Baytown residents with trustworthy local resources

Our Mission

To connect Baytown residents with resources and services that can assist them.

We have noticed that most community information was difficult to find and spread out across various websites.

This hub puts everything in one place for our community's convenience so people don't have to scour the internet for community resources.

Our Goals

  • Make resources easy to access
  • Keep information accurate
  • Work with local organizations
  • Improve the site over time

Why We Built This Hub

Why this project was needed

The Problem

People don't always know where to go when they need help in their community.

The Solution

One website that organizes local resources in a clear and accessible way.

The Impact

Faster access to information and stronger community connections.

How the Hub Works

Basic steps for using the website

Step 1

Step 2

Read information about the organization.

Step 3

Step 4

Get the help you need.
